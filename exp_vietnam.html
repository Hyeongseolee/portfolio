Industrial PLC Automation Training for Global Tech Talent

Empowering Next-Generation Engineers Through Knowledge Sharing

PLC Ladder Logic Sequence Control Technical Education

🔹Context & Objective

Overview: A global technical volunteer initiative aimed at delivering industrial automation education to technology students in Vietnam.

Objective: Bridge the educational gap in practical automation by engineering a comprehensive, hands-on PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) curriculum.

🔹Core Activities & Impact

1. Curriculum Engineering

Developed a step-by-step training framework covering classical Sequence Control theory, industrial sensor wiring, and `Ladder Logic` programming.

2. Instructional Leadership

Served as the Main Instructor, delivering technical lectures and leading laboratory sessions under multilingual environments.

3. Project Mentorship

Guided student teams through capstone-style lab exercises, such as programming a miniature automated conveyor sorting system.

🔹Key Takeaways

  • Deepened personal mastery of industrial automation standards and sequential logic design by teaching them.
  • Cultivated exceptional cross-cultural leadership, patience, and empathetic technical communication skills.
